Shanny Dyer

Chairperson & Managing Director

Shanny is a leader and entrepreneur who has extensive experience in building businesses linking industry, government and universities. Well respected in both Federal and State government and seen as a corporate innovator.

She holds key roles in a number of companies focussed on medical development, including Seagull Technologies which specialises in non-invasive drug delivery systems and Bionic Vision Australia to assist with commercial focus and growth.

Her strong history in the sector includes roles in Australia’s widely recognised CSIRO as GM Commercial Governance and Performance Management and Deputy Head of the Centre for Clinical Genomics, Garvan institute.
