Wavefront solves the problem

of uniquely identifying a person securely in a way that is very hard to fool. Wavefront works specifically to incorporate liveness​ and captures multi-modal biometric information in one pass. All this in close to real time. Simply, quickly, with low user effort.

This revolutionary biometric technology has been developed over an 8 year period by some of the leading innovators in the world of corneal research and optometry and is now being tested for use with laptops, tablets, mobile devices and specific identification and validation solutions.

Wavefront's patented solution delivers

  • the discovery of the world’s first multi-modal biometric using live corneal and iris analysis to provide unique identification
  • the discovery of a new method using reflected white light to enable enrollment of all eyes
  • a new benchmark for one sensor efficiency with a single biometric pass harvesting multiple identifying traits
  • the related software and algorithms that can measure and record over the span of a lifetime minute changes in the biometric markers for each eye
  • the ongoing innovation and development of software and test hardware to validate adaptability for multiple platforms
  • continual discovery of new eye-related measures and markers to deepen biometric accuracy
