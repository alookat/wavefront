Arun Bhan

Commercial Advisor

The Managing Partner of Dublin based, global business consultancy Matrix Advisors, and a Global Advisor to both the United Nations Office of Global Development (UNDP) and the United Nations Office for Project Services (UNOP), Arun Bhan is currently an advisor to the governments of the US, Japan, India and Ireland, on strategic direction for business and the global challenges faced by the international business community.

As an executive board member of the India Japan Global Partnership, director of the Ireland India Council and member of the Irish Global Economic Forum and Ireland US Council, Arun has a deep understanding of both challenges and opportunities.

A member and global advisor for more than 20 Boards of major corporations, business associations and community organizations throughout the world, Arun has a lifelong commitment to help commercialize new inventions and innovations for ICT (Information and Communications Technology), Life Sciences and Sustainable Technology that can change market paradigms.
